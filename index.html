PRESERVING* save 954 Highland

954 Highland has served as a central hub of Black culture for generations. Our mission is to memorialize that history and build upon it — a place where the community can come together and reconnect with the dream Dr. King preached a few buildings away.
Current status: under new ownership and still at risk of demolition.
